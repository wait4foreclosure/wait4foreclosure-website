Explore your options. Stay in control.

Whether you want to stay in your home or move on with the least financial impact, we can guide you toward the right solution.

Loan Modification

Permanently change your loan terms to lower your monthly payments to an affordable level.

Forbearance Plans

Temporarily pause or reduce your payments if you're facing a short-term financial hardship.

Repayment Plans

Catch up on past-due payments over an extended period without a lump-sum payment.

Short Sale

Sell your home for less than you owe, with the lender's approval, avoiding foreclosure.

Deed in Lieu

Voluntarily transfer the property deed to the lender to satisfy your debt and avoid a public auction.

HUD Counseling

We connect you with free, government-approved counselors to explore all available programs.

Our Mission: A Fresh Start for Homeowners

Wait4Foreclosure was founded on the belief that everyone deserves a fighting chance to keep their home. Financial hardship can happen to anyone, and navigating the complex world of mortgage lending is daunting.

We are a team of dedicated housing advocates who work for you, not the banks. We'll be your partner, advocating on your behalf to secure a positive resolution and help you achieve the peace of mind that comes with a stable home.
